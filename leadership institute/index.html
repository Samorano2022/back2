About the Institute President
Rev. Samson Ajetomobi is the President and Founder of the Men of Issachar Vision Inc., demonstrating his initiative and leadership in establishing and leading an organization dedicated to impacting church leaders and reaching the unreached.
Chairman of PFN Oyo State Chapter: Serving as the chairman of the PFN Oyo State Chapter further highlights Rev. Ajetomobi's leadership credentials, indicating his trusted leadership role within the Pentecostal Fellowship of Nigeria at the state level.
Leadership Involvement: His involvement with the leadership of several Christian organizations and mission agencies underscores his recognition and influence within the Christian community, as well as his ability to collaborate and lead effectively in diverse contexts.
Author and Speaker: As an author of several books and a speaker at conferences, crusades, and church revivals, Rev. Ajetomobi demonstrates his ability to articulate vision, teach, and inspire others to action through his written and spoken words.
Missionary Heart: Rev. Ajetomobi's missionary zeal and passion for souls reflect his commitment to fulfilling the Great Commission and leading others in evangelism and discipleship efforts.
